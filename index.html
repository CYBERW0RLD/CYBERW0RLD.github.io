Deltarune is a cool and fun RPG game that'll definitely make you become a big shot in a short time! Bask into the fantastical worlds you, kris, susie, and ralsei explore, all while facing incredibly challenging enemies that'll test your determination. You can also meet wonderfully charismatic characters along the way as well!

Deltarune is a parallel to Undertale, a game developed by the same creator: Toby Fox. The demo of Deltarune is currently downloadable on Steam.
